Practice Problems

Unconstrained Optimization

Basic: Optimality Conditions

Define local and global optima and explain first-order optimality conditions.

Intermediate: Second Derivatives

Apply second-order conditions to classify critical points as maxima or minima.

Advanced: Convexity Analysis

Analyze convexity properties and their relationship to optimization.

Constrained Optimization

Basic: Lagrange Multipliers

Define Lagrange multipliers and explain their geometric interpretation.

Intermediate: KKT Conditions

Apply Karush-Kuhn-Tucker conditions to solve constrained optimization problems.

Advanced: Constraint Qualifications

Analyze different constraint qualification conditions and their implications.

Gradient Methods

Basic: Steepest Descent

Define steepest descent method and explain its convergence properties.

Intermediate: Newton's Method

Apply Newton's method and analyze its quadratic convergence rate.

Advanced: Conjugate Gradient

Implement conjugate gradient method for large-scale optimization problems.

Stochastic Optimization

Basic: Expected Value

Define expected value optimization and explain stochastic optimization.

Intermediate: Stochastic Gradient

Apply stochastic gradient descent and analyze variance reduction techniques.

Advanced: Convergence Analysis

Prove convergence properties of stochastic optimization algorithms.

Convex Optimization

Basic: Convex Sets

Define convex sets and functions and explain their properties.

Intermediate: Duality Theory

Apply Lagrange duality to derive dual optimization problems.

Advanced: Strong Duality

Prove strong duality theorems for convex optimization problems.
