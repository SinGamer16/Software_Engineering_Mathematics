Vectors and Vector Spaces

Vector Operations

Addition

\( \vec{a} + \vec{b} = (a_1 + b_1, a_2 + b_2, \dots, a_n + b_n) \)

Scalar Multiplication

\( c\vec{v} = (c v_1, c v_2, \dots, c v_n) \)

Dot Product

\( \vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = a_1 b_1 + a_2 b_2 + \dots + a_n b_n \)

Cross Product (3D)

\( \vec{a} \times \vec{b} = (a_y b_z - a_z b_y, a_z b_x - a_x b_z, a_x b_y - a_y b_x) \)

Matrices and Operations

Matrix Operations

Addition

\( (A + B)_{ij} = A_{ij} + B_{ij} \)

Multiplication

\( (AB)_{ij} = \sum_k A_{ik} B_{kj} \)

Transpose

\( (A^T)_{ij} = A_{ji} \)

Inverse

\( A^{-1} A = A A^{-1} = I \)

Linear Transformations

Definition

A linear transformation T: V ‚Üí W is a function that satisfies:

  • Additivity: T(u + v) = T(u) + T(v)
  • Homogeneity: T(cv) = cT(v)

Every linear transformation has a unique matrix representation.

Common Transformations

Rotation

\( \begin{pmatrix} \cos\theta & -\sin\theta \\ \sin\theta & \cos\theta \end{pmatrix} \)

Scaling

\( \begin{pmatrix} s_x & 0 \\ 0 & s_y \end{pmatrix} \)

Shear

\( \begin{pmatrix} 1 & k \\ 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix} \)

Reflection

\( \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{pmatrix} \)
